Admissions

Explore our admission process and join our vibrant academic community.

Undergraduate Program

Admissions are through a single window system with reservations as per Tamil Nadu Government rules.

Postgraduate Program

Admissions for M.E./M.Tech are through Anna University's Single Window System with TANCET/GATE required.

Ph.D. Program

Ph.D. scholars are selected based on Anna University's list and pursue the program under recognized supervisors.
